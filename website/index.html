Why Act Now

Nothing lasts forever — not even brick and stone.

The durability of masonry is legendary, but in the Upper Midwest, moisture and freeze-thaw cycles are relentless. Small problems become big, costly ones when ignored. Three of the most common:

01

Deteriorating balcony concrete

Water and cold break down flat concrete, loosening railings and endangering residents.

02

Water in mortar joints

In older buildings, water permeates cracks in mortar or brick itself — left alone, whole sections of wall are affected.

03

Failed coping caulk

When caulk deteriorates at coping, water seeps behind the stonework. Prompt recaulking prevents serious damage to the joints between brick and stone.

Your building is one of the largest investments you'll ever make. Well-maintained masonry protects that investment — and makes the right impression. Holton Brothers helps maintain and enhance your building's appearance and value for decades to come.

Since 1974

One of the Midwest's most successful building restoration companies — since February 12, 1974.

Holton Brothers has served the masonry needs of some of the largest, most prestigious, and most historic buildings in Wisconsin and Illinois for over five decades. No project is too large or too small.

Our workforce includes as many as 100 craftsmen, technicians, and specialists — most of whom have been with the company for many years. Our superintendents and sales staff attend trade shows and conventions to stay current on the latest techniques and materials, and our crews work with the best equipment, systems, and the safest known methods.
